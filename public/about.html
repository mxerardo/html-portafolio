About Me

My name is Gerardo, and I'm originally from Guanajuato, Mexico. I'm 35 years old,
and I'm currently in the process of learning how to program. Technology has always
intrigued me, but it was only a few months ago that I decided to take the plunge.
I have a background in industrial engineering and possess 11 years of professional
experience in various industries, including automotive, motorcycling, and even the
healthcare sector..

I enjoy playing the piano, drawing, and attending concerts. However, my greatest pastime
is reading. So far, my favorite books include "The Psychology of Money" by Morgan Housel,
"Sapiens" by Yuval Harari, and "The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks" by author Rebecca Skloot.
